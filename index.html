About

The Math and Science Club (MaSC) at Southwestern College is an interdisciplinary group of students with varying interest. MaSC members work on STEM-based projects that focus on learning to use the tools of the future. Some of our recent workshops include Cyber Security, UX Design and Electrical Engineering. We want to provide a platfrom for curious and dedicated inviduals to explore their field of interest and flourish as both a students and an independent thinkers.
